Agada Blessed Obutu, also known popularly by the stage name Berri Tiga, is a young fast rising Nigerian song writer and singer. Born on the 10th of October 2004, he hails from Benue state, although he spent most of his life in Lagos. Agada Blessed Obutu started his music career at a very young age. He attended French village International College in Badagry, Lagos State. There, he was always writing and singing songs he wrote to his friends. A few years later, a freestyle video he posted saw him gain attention from various sources, the freestyle was titled Jo". Later on he was noticed and then signed into his former label, Big Brother Music. He became well-known in 2021 thanks to his consistent cover-making, earning him a record deal with his former Big Brother Management (BBR). When Berri Tiga was included on Efe Carter's smash single "Machala" in 2022, his career took off. Several music charts, including Apple Music's, quickly added the song to the top of their lists. Their collaboration, "Machala," reached number one on multiple charts, and his music has been streamed millions of times across multiple platforms. Berri Tiga has released three songs titled MMBM(Money Must Be Made), GOD, MACHALA under his former record label Big Brother Music. Driven by the desire to connect with his fans and inspire other, Berri Tiga enter an agreement with the Big Brother Music to be his management. In addition to the management deal, Berri Tiga also secured an exclusive distribution deal. This partnership provides him means to share his music to a wider audience.
